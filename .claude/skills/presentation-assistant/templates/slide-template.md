## Design Specifications

- **Font**: Arial or Helvetica (body), Georgia (title)
- **Font Sizes**: 
  - Title: 32pt
  - Subheading: 24pt
  - Body: 18pt minimum
- **Margins**: 1 inch minimum on all sides
- **Colors**: 
  - Background: White (default) or Dark Blue (emphasis)
  - Text: Black (on white) or White (on dark)
  - Accent: Blue shades (consistent across presentation)
  - Green: Positive values/increases only
  - Red: Negative values/decreases only
  - Gray: De-emphasized information
- **Maximum Colors**: 3-5 total per presentation
- **Presentation Time**: 60 seconds maximum

---

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Action title states complete, specific conclusion
- [ ] Slide communicates exactly one message
- [ ] All elements support only that message
- [ ] Content provides insight, not just information
- [ ] No forbidden terminology (MECE, Pyramid Principle, SCQA, etc.)
- [ ] Language is natural business communication
- [ ] Visual design follows minimalist principles
- [ ] Fonts, margins, and alignments are consistent
- [ ] All data points are sourced and attributed
- [ ] Can be presented in 60 seconds
