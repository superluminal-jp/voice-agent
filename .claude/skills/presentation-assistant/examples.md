# Presentation Assistant - Examples

## Example 1: Single Slide Creation

**User**: "Create a slide showing that our Q3 revenue increased 18%"

**Assistant Output**:

```markdown
## Action Title
Q3 revenue increased 18% to $12.5M driven by new customer acquisition and product line expansion

## Body Content

### Chart
- **Chart Type**: Column chart
- **Data Series**: 
  - Q2 2024: $10.6M
  - Q3 2024: $12.5M
- **Key Message**: 18% quarter-over-quarter growth
- **Color Coding**: 
  - Q2: Gray
  - Q3: Blue (accent)
- **Callouts**: 
  - **$12.5M** (bold, larger font)
  - **+18%** (bold, green)

### Supporting Points
1. **New customers**: Added 450 enterprise accounts (35% increase)
2. **Product expansion**: Premium tier adoption grew 42%
3. **Market position**: Now #2 in market share (up from #4)

## Source Citation
Source: Internal Financial Reports, Q3 2024

## Design Notes
- Use blue accent color for Q3 column
- Bold the $12.5M figure and +18% growth
- Keep chart simple with only 2 data points
- White background, black text
```

## Example 2: Executive Summary Slide

**User**: "Create an executive summary slide for our market expansion strategy"

**Assistant Output**:

```markdown
## Action Title
Entering Southeast Asian markets represents $2.5B opportunity requiring $15M investment over 18 months

## Subheading
Market analysis shows strong demand with limited competition in target segments

## Body Content

### Supporting Points
1. **Market size**: $2.5B addressable market across Indonesia, Thailand, and Philippines
2. **Competitive position**: Only 2 established players, both with limited digital capabilities
3. **Investment required**: $15M over 18 months for market entry
4. **Expected returns**: Break-even by month 24, $50M revenue by year 3

### Key Metrics (Bold)
- **$2.5B** total addressable market
- **$15M** investment required
- **18 months** to market entry
- **24 months** to break-even

## Source Citation
Source: Market Research Report, Strategy Team Analysis, Q4 2024

## Design Notes
- Use blue for positive metrics
- Bold all key figures
- Keep layout clean with generous white space
- Maximum 2 lines for action title
```

## Example 3: Data-Driven Slide

**User**: "Create a slide showing customer satisfaction improvement"

**Assistant Output**:

```markdown
## Action Title
Customer satisfaction scores increased from 72 to 86 following service redesign, exceeding industry benchmark of 81

## Body Content

### Chart
- **Chart Type**: Line chart showing trend over time
- **Data Series**: 
  - Q1 2024: 72
  - Q2 2024: 75
  - Q3 2024: 82
  - Q4 2024: 86
- **Key Message**: Steady improvement with significant jump in Q3
- **Color Coding**: 
  - Our scores: Blue line
  - Industry benchmark: Gray dashed line at 81
- **Callouts**: 
  - **86** (current score, bold, larger)
  - **+14 points** improvement (green, bold)
  - Benchmark line clearly marked

### Supporting Points
1. **Service redesign**: Implemented in Q3, addressing top 3 complaint categories
2. **Response time**: Reduced from 48 hours to 4 hours average
3. **Resolution rate**: Increased from 65% to 89% first-contact resolution

## Source Citation
Source: Customer Satisfaction Survey, Q1-Q4 2024; Industry Benchmark Report 2024

## Design Notes
- Green for positive change (+14 points)
- Blue line for our performance
- Gray dashed line for benchmark
- Bold the 86 score prominently
```
